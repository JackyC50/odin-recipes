Jack's Secret Pasta Recipe

Description

This recipe has allowed me to gain 30 pounds of muscle without getting bored of this delicious pasta. It will be a valuable asset in your recipe arsenal to becoming the biggest and healthiest version of yourself. I introduce you to one of the keys to your lean bulk journey.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of non-salted butter
  • 2 cups of tomato or marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 224 grams of pasta of your choice
  • 1/2 tbsp of minced garlic
  • 200g of boneless skinless chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsps of italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp of cooking oil
  • 1 tsp of msg
  • 1 cup of broccoli

Steps

  1. Grab a large pot and boil the 10 cups of hot water (use hot water so water will boil faster)
  2. While the water is boiling, cut your chicken thigh into 4 equal pieces and season them with salt
  3. Heat up a skillet on medium high and once hot enough (you will see a bit of smoke), put 2 tbsp of cooking oil on the pan and coat the whole pan
  4. Place your chicken thigh onto the pan and sear each side for 2 minutes
  5. Cook your chicken thigh for another 3 minutes on each side on medium low heat (total cooking time of thigh: 10 minutes)
  6. While you are cooking your chicken, the water should be done boiling, place your uncooked pasta into the boiling water and let it cook for 12-14 minutes (check at 12 minute mark and take out when texture is desired)
  7. Boil your broccoli in another pot of water while pasta is cooking
  8. While your broccoli and pasta are cooking, slice one tbsp of butter, mince your garlic, and prepare the other non-used ingredients
  9. Strain the pasta and the broccoli and then heat up your pot under medium heat
  10. Put 1 tbsp of cooking oil, butter, and garlic in pot
  11. Put your tomato or marinara sauce in pot
  12. Put msg, red pepper flakes, heavy cream, sugar, and italian seasoning in it while continuously stirring
  13. Put the pasta and the chicken thigh in the new sauce and place the mozerlla cheese in
  14. Stir the pasta around and cook until all the cheese is melted
  15. Enjoy this delicious meal
