Jack's Secret Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This recipe is one of my favorites as it represents who I am, a mix of absolutely everything. Fried rice is delicious and you can practically add anything to it and it will taste great. Here is my take on fried rice for gains. This is for a serving for 3 people or 3 meals for you.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of jasmine rice
  • 300 g of chicken thigh
  • 4 tbsp of sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of msg
  • 1 large bell pepper
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 4 tbsp of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice (make sure rice is not too soft) (1 cup water for every 1 cup of rice)
  2. Cook chicken thigh in a skillet
  3. Bring out the Wok or a large pan or pot
  4. Put in 2 tbsp of oil on high heat and place in your 3 large eggs and once half cooked (still runny), place it in a bowl on the side
  5. Put in 1 tbsp of oil and cook your minced bell pepper on medium heat
  6. Once the bell peppers are cooked, place in minced garlic with one more tbsp of cooking oil
  7. Place in your 3 cups of cooked rice and break apart your rice
  8. Put in soy sauce and msg
  9. Put in sesame oil and stir well
  10. Place egg back in and put cooked chicken thigh on top of your fried rice
  11. Enjoy this magnificent dish
